# Enterprise E-Commerce Sales Analytics Platform

## Project Objective

Develop an end-to-end analytics platform that tracks e-commerce sales data, stores it in PostgreSQL, and visualizes business KPIs through an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

---

## Technologies

- Python
- PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- SQL
- Pandas
- Streamlit
- Plotly
- Faker
- SQLAlchemy

---

## Project Modules

### Executive Dashboard
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Total Customers

### Sales Analytics
- Revenue Trend
- Category Performance
- Monthly Sales

### Customer Analytics
- Top Customers
- Customer Growth

### Product Analytics
- Top Products
- Category Analysis

---

## Expected Outcome

A production-style analytics platform that simulates how an e-commerce company tracks business performance.
